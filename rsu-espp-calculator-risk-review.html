üìö Tax Calculation Background

  • RSU Tax Timing:
    • At Vesting: Taxed as ordinary income at market price (already paid)
    • At Sale: Capital gains tax on appreciation from vesting price to sale price
  • ESPP Taxation: The discount amount is typically taxed as ordinary income. Additional gain from purchase price to sale price may be capital gains.
  • Tax Rate Differences: Ordinary income taxed at higher federal rates (up to 37%) vs. long-term capital gains (0%, 15%, or 20%).
  • State Considerations: States like California add significant tax burden (up to 13.3% additional).
  • AMT Impact: Alternative Minimum Tax can significantly affect ESPP transactions.
  • Calculator Note: This tool calculates only additional tax liability from your requested liquidation. RSU vesting taxes were already paid when shares vested.
